Summary

I am a motivated and independent Fashion Design Student currently pursuing an Associate in Applied Science in Fashion Design at the Fashion Institute of Technology in New York. As a self-driven fashion design student, I am eager to apply my skills and knowledge to real-world challenges through an internship role.

My design philosophy centers around creating mood boards with thorough research and brand collaboration. I've worked on projects involving brands like DKNY and Kate Spade to show calm and effervescence in the form of soft aesthetic into the big noisy city life.

I'm passionate about visual illustration, researching fashion forecast trends, and experimenting with draping techniques. My educational background includes studies at Podar International School in Mumbai, where I focused on Psychology, Business, Art and Design, English, French, and Economics.
