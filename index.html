Summary

I'm a passionate and independent Fashion Design student studying at the Fashion Institute of Technology (FIT) in New York. Right now, I'm working on my Associate degree and will continue with my Bachelor's in Fashion Design.

I love bringing ideas to life through draping, patternmaking, digital design, and visual design. I always start with strong research and aim to create designs that tell a story. One of my favorite projects was a mix of DKNY and Kate Spade, where I explored soft and calm designs inspired by big city life.

This summer, I worked at Gerber Childrenswear LLC as a Design and Technical Design Intern. I helped the team with things like making tech packs, fitting clothes using muslin, creating digital sketches in Adobe Illustrator, and reviewing samples. I also used a system called BlueCherry to track product details and worked with teams in China to manage updates and timelines. I'm always excited to learn more, be part of creative teams, and grow as a designer by working on real-world projects.
